Duo Peng is currently a Tenured Associate Professor and PhD Supervisor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Tongji University, and a member of the team led by Prof. Heng Tao Shen. He was selected for a national-level young talent program (Overseas).

His research interests include domain adaptation and generalization, computer vision, and embodied intelligence, with a particular focus on building robust and adaptive intelligent systems for open-world environments.

In recent years, he has published 20+ papers in leading international journals and conferences in artificial intelligence and computer vision, including IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TIP, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ICML, and NeurIPS, with 10+ first-author/corresponding-author papers. His representative works were selected for Oral presentations at CVPR 2022 and ECCV 2024, and received the PREMIA Best Student Paper Award (Silver Award).

He has served as a core member in several high-level Singapore research projects, including those funded by NRF and MOE Tier-2. He also serves as a reviewer and program committee member for major conferences and journals in artificial intelligence and computer vision, and has been invited to serve as a guest editor for international journals.
